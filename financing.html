How we price things

You'll see every number before you agree to anything.

Most dental anxiety isn't really about the drill — it's about not knowing what something will cost until the bill shows up. We don't work that way. Every treatment plan is itemized, dated, and handed to you in writing during your consultation, before any work is ever scheduled. Nothing gets added later that wasn't already on that page.

If your plan changes mid-treatment — say, a tooth needs something extra once we're already in there — we stop, explain why, and get your approval and a new written number before continuing. You'll never wake up from a procedure to a bill that doesn't match what you agreed to.

Insurance coordination

We speak insurance so you don't have to.

Dental insurance is notoriously confusing, and most plans only cover a fraction of cosmetic or implant work anyway. Rather than leaving you to decode an Explanation of Benefits on your own, our team handles the entire process — so you know your real out-of-pocket number before treatment ever starts.

  • We verify your benefits before your first appointment, not after.
  • Our team files every claim directly with your insurer on your behalf.
  • If you're out-of-network, we help you maximize reimbursement instead of assuming you have none.
  • You'll know your estimated out-of-pocket portion before treatment starts — not after a confusing EOB arrives.
Common financing questions

The questions everyone's a little embarrassed to ask out loud.

Will applying for financing hurt my credit score?

Most pre-qualification checks, including CareCredit's, use a soft inquiry that doesn't affect your score. A hard inquiry only happens if you move forward with a full application, and we'll always tell you which type a lender is running before you apply.

What if I've been denied financing somewhere else before?

Different lenders use different approval criteria, and a denial from one doesn't predict the outcome with another. Our coordinators can usually identify which financing partner is the best fit for your situation before you formally apply.

Can I use my dental insurance and financing together?

Yes — most patients do. We apply your insurance benefit first, then finance only the remaining out-of-pocket balance, so you're never financing money your insurance was already going to cover.

Is there a minimum credit score required?

It depends on the lender and plan. CareCredit considers a range of factors beyond a single score, and our in-house plans are flexible because they're built around your specific budget rather than a fixed approval formula.

What happens if I pay off my plan early?

With CareCredit and our installment-loan partners, there's no prepayment penalty — paying early, or in full, only reduces the total interest you pay over the life of the loan.

Do you accept HSA or FSA cards directly at checkout?

Yes. We run HSA and FSA cards exactly like a regular debit or credit card at the time of payment, and we'll provide an itemized receipt your plan administrator can use if documentation is ever requested.
