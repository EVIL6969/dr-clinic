Before you book

A few quick answers to common questions.

Do I need a referral to book a consultation?

No referral is needed. You can request a consultation directly through the form above or by phone, whether you're a brand-new patient or returning after time away.

What should I bring to my first visit?

A photo ID, your insurance card if you have one, and a list of any current medications. If you have recent X-rays from another provider, feel free to bring or email those ahead of time.

What if I need to reschedule or cancel?

Just call or text our office at least 24 hours ahead and we'll find a new time that works — no penalty for advance notice. Same-day cancellations may be asked to reschedule for the next available slot.

Do you treat dental emergencies the same day?

Yes. Call our office directly for severe pain, swelling, a broken tooth, or a lost crown — we hold same-day slots specifically for urgent cases.

Will you verify my insurance before I arrive?

Yes — once you submit the form above with your insurance details (or call ahead), our team verifies your benefits in advance so you know your estimated coverage before your visit, not after.
